Why Kitabi

A real bookshelf, finally digital

Reading apps track what you read. Cataloguing apps list what you own. Kitabi does both — and remembers which friend still has your favourite paperback.

📚

A shelf that looks like your shelf

Cover after cover, just like the real thing. Even books without a photo get a handsome cover of their own — your library always looks loved, never empty.

⇄

Never lose a lent bookFREE

“Lent to Anu, 2 June.” Kitabi remembers who has your books and gives you a gentle nudge when it’s time to ask for one back. Your paperbacks come home.

▦

Move in, in minutes

Already keep your books on Goodreads or in a spreadsheet? Bring it along — your shelves, ratings and reviews arrive together, in one tap.

✎

Your reading, remembered

Where you stopped, what you rated, what you really thought — and the little notes you’d never say out loud (“signed copy, don’t lend”). All of it stays yours, private until you decide otherwise.

◔

Your year in pages

Watch your reading add up — books finished, pages turned, the languages on your shelf. A reading journal that fills itself in.

✦

Know what to read next

Warm suggestions drawn from the books you loved, each with an honest “why this one”. Prefer to browse in peace? One switch turns them off.

Reads your language

മലയാളം · தமிழ் · English & beyond

Kitabi grew up on Indian bookshelves. Your മലയാളം, தமிழ் and English books live side by side — spelled right, shown proudly, never an afterthought. And when you own both Mayyazhippuzhayude Theerangalil and On the Banks of the Mayyazhi, Kitabi knows they’re the same story.

❦

Your shelf is waiting

Kitabi arrives soon on Android and iOS — because your books deserve better than a spreadsheet. The door opens right here at kitabi.in.
